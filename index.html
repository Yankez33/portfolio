Hi there. I'm Tim

A detail oriented developer who isn’t willing to accept “ It’s good enough” and always strives for a better end product.

While still learning to be a developer, my strengths lie in the front end but I have a working knowledge of Python and how to apply it in the back end.

I currently reside In Victoria B.C., Canada but long to live in the mountains, where nature is my backyard and a log cabin is my castle.
